Rwanda visa types for Guatemalan citizens:

All foreigners need a visa to visit Rwanda unless they are from a visa-exempt nation.

Tourists, business travelers, and students must apply for a visa to enter Rwanda, even for short stays.

There are 4 types of visas in Rwanda

  • Rwanda tourist e-visas: are valid for 03 months after arrival. You have a single entry and can stay in Rwanda for up to 30 days.
  • Rwanda Conference e-Visa is a travel document available for visitors to Rwanda attending a conference, meeting, or seminar. This visa is valid for 03 months from the date of arrival and allows you to stay in Rwanda for a total of 30 days.
  • Rwanda transit e-visa: is issued to a person who transits through Rwanda. A Rwanda transit visa allows a total stay of 3 days per entry.
  • East Africa Visa: is valid for 03 months after arrival, and allows for multiple entries to Rwanda, Kenya and Uganda. You can stay at your destination for up to 30 days per entry.
